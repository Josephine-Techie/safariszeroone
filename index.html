Mamba Village Visit (Crocodile Farm Tour)

Here you will visit East Africa’s largest crocodile farm.Here you will visit East Africa’s largest crocodile farm. To understand the crocodiles life cycle, there's a display of existing crocodiles weight, length and age. How the multiply and interesting facts about their life that you don't know. You will also enjoy seeing the young crocodiles that are 5 meters long. The man-eating monster can also be seen basking along the beautiful garden trail under the sun.Later in the evening , you will see the crocodiles being fed at a round five which is quite interesting to watch. Besides the crocodiles there's an opportunity to do Camel Riding and Horse Riding. You can have your lunch of unique dishes such as crocodile meat and ostrich at the Mamba restaurants. Do you love plants and flowers? If yes, you will find plenty of this during your tour at the Mamba village as well as snakes and marine aquarium.
